# VAT (BTW) Guide

## VAT Rates in Netherlands

### 21% - Standard Rate
Most services and goods:
- Professional services
- B2B services
- Digital products
- Marketing

### 9% - Reduced Rate
Limited categories:
- Food products
- Books
- Hotel accommodation

### 0% - Zero-rated
- Exports outside EU
- Intra-community supplies

### Vrijgesteld (Exempt)
- Healthcare
- Education
- Financial services

### Verlegd (Reverse Charge)
- B2B to EU businesses (with VAT number)
- B2B outside EU

## Filing Periods

### Quarterly (Most Common)
- Q1: File by Apr 30
- Q2: File by Jul 31
- Q3: File by Oct 31
- Q4: File by Jan 31

## Deductible Expenses

Can deduct VAT on:
- Office supplies
- Software
- Professional services
- Marketing
- Business travel

Cannot deduct:
- Personal expenses
- Entertainment (limited)

## Common Scenarios

### Dutch B2B Client
Charge 21% VAT with your VAT number.

### EU B2B Client
Use reverse charge (0%), verify their VAT number via VIES.

### Consumer in NL
Charge 21% VAT.

### Outside EU
Usually 0% VAT (export).

## Invoice Requirements

Must include:
1. Your VAT number
2. Client details
3. Unique invoice number
4. Date
5. Description
6. Quantity and price
7. VAT rate and amount
8. Total

## Tips

- Record invoices when sent
- Keep receipts 7 years
- Review monthly
- Verify EU VAT numbers
- Consult advisor for complex cases
