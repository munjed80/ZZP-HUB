# Product Overview

## ZZP HUB
ZZP HUB is a premium financial dashboard designed specifically for Dutch freelancers (zzp'ers). It provides a comprehensive solution for managing all aspects of freelance business administration in one place.

## Core Purpose
The platform simplifies financial administration by combining invoicing, quotations, VAT management, time tracking, and reporting into a single, user-friendly interface.

## Key Benefits
- **Time Saving**: Streamline administrative tasks that would typically take hours
- **Professional**: Generate professional invoices and quotations with your company branding
- **Compliance**: Stay compliant with Dutch VAT regulations and the 1225-hour criterion
- **Clarity**: Clear overview of income, expenses, and VAT obligations
- **Mobility**: Access your data anywhere, with offline support for mobile devices

## Target Users
Dutch freelancers (zzp'ers) who need:
- Professional invoice and quotation management
- VAT (BTW) tracking and reporting
- Time tracking for the 1225-hour criterion
- Financial overview and reporting
- Client relationship management
